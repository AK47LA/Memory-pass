## D — DECONSTRUCT (Break Down Into Learnable Units)

**What makes a world-class character IP video?**

### 1. CHARACTER CONSISTENCY (Visual)
- Face proportions (eye size/spacing, snout length, whiskers, thick eyebrows)
- Body proportions (torso/flipper/tail ratio)
- Skin texture (smooth seal skin vs furry)
- Color palette (gray scale, white accents)
- Expressions (happy/sad/surprised range)

### 2. MOVEMENT QUALITY
- Natural motion (seal-like behavior)
- Weight and physics (seal body moves correctly)
- Timing (not too fast, not too slow)
- Gesture library (wave, clap, bounce, spin)

### 3. LIP SYNC
- Mouth shapes match phonemes (a, e, i, o, u, m, th, etc.)
- Timing sync to audio
- Natural jaw movement (not robotic)
- Expression during speech (eyes, eyebrows move with emotion)

### 4. SCENE COMPOSITION
- Camera angles (low/high/eye-level)
- Framing (rule of thirds, negative space)
- Background detail (Room of Wonder elements)
- Lighting (warm California sun, soft shadows)

### 5. STORYTELLING
- Emotional arc (setup → moment → payoff)
- Pacing (beat timing, when to pause)
- Audio sync (music/SFX/voice match visuals)
- Hook (first 3 seconds grab attention)

### 6. TECHNICAL QUALITY
- Resolution (1080p minimum, 4K ideal)
- Frame rate (24/30fps consistency)
- Color grading (warm/cool balance)
- Compression (no artifacts, clean export)
