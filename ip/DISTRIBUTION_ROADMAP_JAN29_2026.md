## YOUTUBE STRATEGY (MARKETING FUNNEL)

### DON'T MONETIZE YOUTUBE — USE IT FOR DISCOVERY

**Upload Strategy:**
- Weeks 1-10 songs: Upload FULL videos (2-3 min) — builds trust, shows quality
- Weeks 11+ songs: Upload TEASERS (30-60 sec) — drives to SVOD platform

**Every Video Includes:**
- Title: "Silly the Seal - [Song Title] (Music for Kids 3-7)"
- Description: "Watch unlimited Silly songs at [your SVOD URL] for $9.99/month. New song every Friday!"
- End card: "Watch more Silly songs at [SVOD URL]"
- Pinned comment: "Parents: Get all Silly songs ad-free at [SVOD URL]"

**YouTube = Free Billboard (3 jobs only):**
1. Prove quality (parents see production value)
2. Build trust (consistent uploads, positive comments)
3. Drive to SVOD (clear CTA in every video)

**DO NOT RELY ON YOUTUBE AD REVENUE ($0.10-0.25 per 1K views = not worth it)**
