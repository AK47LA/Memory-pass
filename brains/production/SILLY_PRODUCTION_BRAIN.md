### Visual Design Tips

**Horizon/Fog Color Rule (via @thomasbrushdev):**
- Make horizon and fog the OPPOSITE color of lights
- Creates gradient of complementary colors
- Adds separation, visual interest, mood
- Source: https://x.com/thomasbrushdev/status/2014675002855993544
