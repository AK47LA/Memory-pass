# SPOTIFY CANVAS WORKFLOW — SIMPLE GUIDE

**What it is:** 3-8 second looping vertical video that plays behind your song in Spotify mobile app

**Why it matters:** Higher engagement, more professional, shows when people listen

---

## 📐 SPECS (MUST FOLLOW EXACTLY)

| Requirement | Value |
|-------------|-------|
| **Aspect ratio** | 9:16 (vertical) |
| **Resolution** | 1080 × 1920 px (1080p) |
| **Duration** | 3-8 seconds (6 sec is safest) |
| **Format** | MP4 (H.264 codec) |
| **File size** | Under 10MB |
| **Frame rate** | 24-30 FPS |

**❌ Don't use:** 16:9 horizontal, square, MOV files, lyrics/text overlays

---

## 🎬 HOW TO CREATE IN CAPCUT (10 MIN PER CANVAS)

### STEP 1: Pick the best 6 seconds from your video

1. Open CapCut Desktop
2. Import your Silly video (Grab The Loop, Taste It Try It, etc.)
3. Find the BEST 6-second moment:
   - Silly's face clearly visible
   - High-energy movement
   - Loops naturally (start looks like end)
4. Mark In/Out points (I and O keys)

---

### STEP 2: Make it vertical + loop-ready

1. **Change canvas:**
   - Click canvas settings
   - Select 9:16 ratio
   - Resolution: 1080 × 1920

2. **Auto-reframe:**
   - Right-click video track → "Auto Reframe"
   - Select 9:16
   - AI centers Silly's face

3. **Test the loop:**
   - Play the 6 seconds on repeat
   - Does start frame match end frame?
   - If not: Trim slightly until it loops smoothly

---

### STEP 3: Keep it clean (no text/effects)

**Canvas rules:**
- ❌ NO lyrics/text overlays
- ❌ NO rapid flashing/strobe effects
- ✅ Just Silly moving/dancing/playing
- ✅ Subtle motion, clean visuals

**Optional:**
- Light color grade (if your video already has it)
- Keep it simple (Canvas is background, music is foreground)

---

### STEP 4: Export correctly

**CapCut export settings:**
1. Click "Export"
2. **Resolution:** 1080p (1920×1080 will auto-rotate to 1080×1920)
3. **Frame rate:** 30 FPS
4. **Format:** MP4
5. **Quality:** High (but check file size after)
6. **Filename:** `Canvas_GrabTheLoop.mp4` (clear naming)

**After export:**
- Check file size (must be under 10MB)
- If over 10MB: Re-export at Medium quality

## 📤 HOW TO UPLOAD (VIA SPOTIFY FOR ARTISTS)

**When you have Spotify for Artists access:**

1. Go to **artists.spotify.com**
2. Click your artist profile (Silly The Seal)
3. Go to **Music** → Select your song
4. Find **Canvas** section
5. Click **Upload Canvas**
6. Upload your MP4 file
7. Preview it (make sure loop looks good)
8. Submit for approval

**Approval time:** Usually 24-48 hours
