# SILLY CAPCUT WORKFLOW - MTV POP-PUNK STYLE

**The repeatable CapCut process for every Silly video.**

---

## THE ORDER (Follow This Every Time)

### PHASE 1: FOUNDATION
**1. Import Audio First**
- Drag song into CapCut
- Everything syncs to the music (audio = spine of the video)

**2. Add Beat Markers**
- Play through and hit "M" on every major beat
- These guide where transitions/effects happen
- Mark: Intro, verse start, chorus hits, breakdown, outro

**3. Lay Out Raw Clips (ONE Track)**
- Import all video clips (from Higgsfield/Kling)
- Line them up on ONE timeline track
- No effects, no text, no transitions yet
- Just raw video rolling to the beat
- Sync each clip to beat markers
- Adjust clip timing/speed if needed to hit beats

---

### PHASE 2: LYRICS/CAPTIONS
**4. Add Lyrics (Manual)**
- Type each line as separate text
- Sync to audio (when the line is sung)
- Use beat markers as guides

**Style Guide (MTV Pop-Punk):**
- **Font:** Bold, sans-serif (Impact, Bebas Neue, or CapCut "Bold")
- **Color:** White text, black stroke (or teal/yellow for Silly branding)
- **Animation:** Pop in on beat, bounce slightly
- **Position:** Bottom third (like karaoke/TikTok style)
- **Size:** Big enough to read on phone

**CapCut Settings:**
- Text → Style → Choose bold preset
- Animation → "Pop" or "Bounce"
- Stroke: 8-10px black
- Align all text the same way (center)

---

### PHASE 3: TRANSITIONS
**5. Add Transitions Between Clips**

**MTV Pop-Punk Transition Pack (Use These Every Time):**
- **Hard cuts** on beat (most common - 70% of transitions)
- **Whip pan** (fast swipe) on big beat changes
- **Zoom in/out** on chorus hits
- **Glitch** (quick digital distortion) for energy bursts
- **Flash** (white flash) on major moments

**CapCut Transitions to Use:**
- Basic → "Dissolve" (0.1s - very fast)
- Camera → "Whip" or "Zoom"
- Glitch → "RGB Split" or "Signal Noise"
- Light → "Flash" (white)

**Rule:** Keep transitions FAST (0.1-0.3 seconds). MTV style = snappy, not smooth.

---

### PHASE 4: EFFECTS (The MTV Pop-Punk Look)
**6. Add Visual Effects**

**The Core MTV Effect Stack (Use on EVERY Video):**

**A. Overlays (Applied to Whole Video):**
- **Light leaks** (subtle warm glow - 20% opacity)
- **Film grain** (adds texture - 15% opacity)
- **VHS scanlines** (optional - 10% for retro vibe)

**CapCut Path:** Effects → Overlays → "Light Leak" / "Film Grain" / "VHS"

**B. Color Correction (Before Color Grading):**
- Boost contrast slightly (+10)
- Increase saturation (+15)
- Warm temperature (+5)

**C. Beat-Synced Effects (On Major Beats):**
- **Zoom punch** (quick zoom in/out on beat - 0.2s)
- **RGB split** (color separation flash - on big moments)
- **Shake** (camera shake on heavy beats)
- **Lens flare** (sun flare on "big" moments)

**CapCut Path:** Effects → Video Effects → "Zoom," "RGB," "Shake," "Flare"

**D. Silly Branding Elements:**
- **Teal accent flashes** (quick teal overlay on transitions)
- **Cartoon pop** effects (stars, sparkles on wins/celebrations)
- **Skate graphics** (optional - skateboard/Venice Beach vibes)

**CapCut Path:** Stickers → Search "cartoon," "skate," "star"

---

### PHASE 5: COLOR GRADING
**7. Apply Color Grade (Final Polish)**

**The Silly Color Grade (Consistent Look):**

**Preset Style:** Warm, vibrant, slightly desaturated shadows (think Venice Beach sunset vibes)

**CapCut Adjustments (Adjust Tab):**
- **Brightness:** +5
- **Contrast:** +15
- **Saturation:** +20 (punchy colors)
- **Temperature:** +10 (warm, golden)
- **Highlights:** -10 (prevent blowout)
- **Shadows:** +10 (lift blacks slightly)
- **Sharpness:** +15 (crisp edges)

**Or Use a Preset:**
- CapCut Filters → "Warm" or "Retro" or "Film"
- Adjust opacity to 60-80% (don't overdo it)

**Save as Custom Preset:** "Silly MTV Style" so you can apply it to every video.

---

### PHASE 6: FINAL TOUCHES
**8. Sound Design (Optional but Recommended)**
- Add subtle **whoosh** sounds on transitions
- Add **punch** sound on beat hits
- Keep volume LOW (5-10%) - music is the star

**9. Review & Export**
- Play through at full speed
- Check sync (beats, lyrics, effects all hit together)
- Export: 1080p, 30fps, High Quality

- [ ] Light leak + film grain overlays applied

### 3. THE "MTV POP-PUNK" VISUAL LANGUAGE

**What makes it MTV, not generic kids video:**

✅ **Fast cuts** (average shot: 2-4 seconds, not 8-10)
✅ **High contrast** (bright brights, dark darks)
✅ **Warm color grade** (golden hour, not flat/clinical)
✅ **Beat-locked everything** (transitions/effects ON the beat, not between)
✅ **Rebellious energy** (glitches, shakes, imperfect = good)
✅ **Text that MOVES** (static text = boring, bouncing text = punk)

❌ **NOT MTV:**
- Smooth, slow transitions (too polished)
- Pastel colors (too soft)
- Clean, perfect cuts (too corporate)
- Text that just sits there (too formal)

**Reference:** Watch Blink-182 "All The Small Things" music video - that's the vibe.

---

### 4. KEYBOARD SHORTCUTS (Learn These)

**Speed up editing 3x:**
- **M** = Add beat marker (while playing)
- **Space** = Play/pause
- **Cmd+Z** = Undo (obvious but critical)
- **Cmd+D** = Duplicate clip/effect
- **J/K/L** = Reverse/pause/forward (scrubbing)
- **I/O** = Set in/out points (for trimming)

**Use these constantly. Clicking menus = slow.**

---

### 5. BATCH YOUR WORK (Don't Do One Thing at a Time)

**Faster workflow:**

**Phase 1-3 (Foundation → Lyrics → Transitions):** Do in ONE session
- Get the bones of the video done
- Save, take a break

**Phase 4-6 (Effects → Color → Final):** Do in NEXT session
- Polish with fresh eyes
- Easier to see what's missing

**Don't do:** Add one effect, review, add another effect, review (too slow)

**Do:** Add ALL beat effects at once, THEN review the whole thing

---

### 6. MOBILE PREVIEW (Parents Watch on Phones)

**Before you call it done:**
- Export a draft
- **AirDrop to your phone**
- Watch it full screen on mobile
- Check:
  * Can you read the text? (If not, make it bigger)
  * Do effects feel too busy on small screen? (Dial back)
  * Is the color grade too dark? (Brighten +5)
  * Does it hold attention? (If you get bored, kids will too)

**Desktop preview ≠ mobile reality.** Always check on phone.

---

### 7. AVOID THESE MISTAKES

**Common traps I've seen:**

❌ **Too many effects** - More ≠ better. Pick 3-4 core effects, use them well.
❌ **Transitions that don't hit beats** - If a transition lands between beats, it feels wrong.
❌ **Text too small** - If you have to squint on desktop, it's invisible on mobile.
❌ **Inconsistent color grade** - Some clips warm, some cool = looks amateur.
❌ **Dead air at the start** - Hook them in 2 seconds, not 8.
❌ **Forgetting to save templates** - You'll rebuild the same text style 50 times otherwise.

---

### 8. THE "IS THIS DONE?" CHECKLIST

**Before you export, ask:**

- [ ] Would I watch this on mute and still feel the energy? (Visual rhythm matters)
- [ ] Does every beat have SOMETHING happening? (Cut, effect, text pop, etc.)
- [ ] Would this fit in an MTV playlist from 2002? (Pop-punk era aesthetic)
- [ ] Can I read all text on my phone? (Mobile-first)
- [ ] Is there a "wow" moment in the first 5 seconds? (Hook them fast)
- [ ] Does it feel like SILLY? (Pop-punk, not generic kids content)

**If any answer is "no," keep polishing.**
