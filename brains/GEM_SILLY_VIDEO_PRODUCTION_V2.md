## 🎤 MOTION CONTROL WORKFLOW (Lip Sync)

### **THE FREEPIK METHOD:**

**STEP 1: Record Your Performance (2-5 minutes)**

1. Play song track on speakers/headphones
2. Record yourself lip syncing on phone camera
3. Exaggerate mouth movements (helps AI)
4. Include body movement (head bobs, gestures)
5. Film in good lighting (AI needs to see your face)

**Tips:**
- Don't worry about looking silly (AI only uses motion data)
- Multiple takes OK (pick best)
- Record slightly wider than needed (crop later)

---

**STEP 2: Upload to Freepik Motion Control**

1. Go to Freepik Motion Control tool
2. Upload your lip sync video
3. Upload Silly character reference image (from scene generation)
4. Set motion strength (80-100% for lip sync)
5. Generate

**Processing time:** 5-15 minutes per video

---

**STEP 3: Download Result**

- AI clones your mouth movement onto Silly's face
- Perfect lip sync timing (matches audio exactly)
- Body movement included (natural performance)
- Unlimited iterations (regenerate if not perfect)

**Cost:** $0 (Freepik Motion Control is free)

---

**STEP 4: Repeat for Each Shot**

- Same lip sync performance works for all angles
- Just change character reference image (different angle)
- Maintains consistency across shots

**Total time:** 30-60 min for complete video (vs 2-4 hours manual)

## 🎞️ VIDEO ASSEMBLY (CapCut)

### **EDITING WORKFLOW:**

**STEP 1: Import Assets**
- All Motion Control videos (3-15 shots)
- Audio track (song)
- Transition effects (optional)

---

**STEP 2: Rough Cut (15-20 minutes)**

1. Place shots on timeline in order

**STEP 3: Add Polish (10-15 minutes)**

- Text overlays (song title, lyrics if teaching)

---

**Settings:**
- Resolution: 1080p (minimum) or 4K

- 16:9 (YouTube)
