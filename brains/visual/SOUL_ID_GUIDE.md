## BEST PRACTICES FOR SOUL ID TRAINING

1. **Consistency across all 20 images** — Same character design in every image. Don't mix styles.
2. **Clear backgrounds for at least 10** — Helps the model isolate the character from environment.
3. **Multiple angles required** — Front, side, back, 3/4. The model needs to understand the character in 3D space.
4. **Include close-ups AND full body** — Model needs to learn both face details and body proportions.
5. **Same outfit in all images** — Don't change the costume. The vest, top, and sneakers are Silly's identity.
6. **High resolution** — At least 1024x1024, ideally 2048x2048.
7. **Good lighting** — Consistent, well-lit. Avoid harsh shadows that obscure details.
8. **No text/watermarks** — Clean images only.
