## COMMON TECHNIQUES FOR SILLY

### CAMERA ANGLES

- **Dutch angle:** Tilted frame (playful, dynamic)
- **Low angle:** Camera below subject (Silly looks bigger, confident)
- **High angle:** Camera above subject (Silly looks smaller, cute)
- **Eye level:** Neutral, relatable

### LIGHTING

- **Chiaroscuro:** High contrast, dramatic shadows
- **Golden hour:** Warm, soft morning/evening light (perfect for Venice Beach)
- **Soft focus:** Dreamy, gentle (Room of Wonder)
- **Rim lighting:** Light around edge of subject (separation from background)

### FOCUS TECHNIQUES

- **Rack focus:** Shift focus from foreground to background (or vice versa)
- **Shallow depth of field:** Blurry background, subject sharp (professional look)
- **Deep focus:** Everything in frame sharp (busy Venice Beach scenes)

### MOVEMENT

- **Tracking shot:** Camera follows subject (Silly walking)
- **Dolly zoom:** Camera moves while zooming (Vertigo effect, dramatic reveals)
- **Pan:** Camera rotates horizontally (survey Venice Beach scene)
- **Tilt:** Camera rotates vertically (follow Silly looking up/down)

### COMPOSITION

- **Rule of thirds:** Subject off-center (more dynamic)
- **Symmetrical framing:** Centered, balanced (formal moments)
- **Leading lines:** Use boardwalk lines to guide eye to Silly
- **Negative space:** Empty space around Silly (emphasize isolation or scale)
