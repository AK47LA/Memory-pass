- Reference image is the source of truth. Never invent features.

## What NOT to do
- Don't describe the character's outfit, fur, stickers etc. — the reference image handles that.
- Don't add walking, sliding, scene transitions, camera push-ins.
- Don't over-direct facial expressions — keep it to one subtle note per beat.
- Don't add background details that aren't in the reference.
- Don't add music or sound effects unless specifically asked.
